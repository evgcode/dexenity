# Product Overview

## What is Dexenity?

Dexenity is an investment research platform that transforms financial data into structured insights for long-term decision making.

## Purpose

The platform supports systematic investment research by:

- Processing public financial data
- Applying consistent analysis methods
- Generating explainable insights
- Enabling reproducible research

## Approach

Dexenity evaluates investments through multiple analytical perspectives:

- **Fundamental Analysis**: Evaluating financial statement quality and business fundamentals
- **Technical Analysis**: Analyzing price and volume patterns
- **Change Detection**: Identifying structural shifts in business performance
- **Context Analysis**: Understanding market environment and conditions

All analysis is deterministic and explainable, not black-box predictions.

## Research Workflow

The platform supports structured research processes:

1. Define investment criteria
2. Apply systematic analysis
3. Filter and rank opportunities
4. Review supporting evidence
5. Validate findings

Research processes are reproducible and auditable.

## Key Principles

### Determinism

Identical inputs produce identical outputs, enabling reliable backtesting and validation.

### Explainability

Every insight includes supporting data and reasoning, allowing verification and understanding.

### Reproducibility

Research processes can be repeated and validated, supporting disciplined decision making.

## What Dexenity Is Not

To set appropriate expectations:

- **Not a trading platform**: Research tool, not an execution system
- **Not financial advice**: Provides analysis, not recommendations
- **Not fully automated**: Human judgment remains essential
- **Not a black box**: All analysis is explainable

## Current Focus

The platform currently focuses on:

- U.S. equity markets
- Public financial data sources
- Systematic analysis methods
- Research tool development

## Use Cases

Dexenity is designed for investors who value:

- Systematic, data-driven research
- Reproducible analysis processes
- Explainable investment insights
- Quality-validated data
